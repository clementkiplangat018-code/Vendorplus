How VendorPlus Works
Agent-verified payments in 4 simple steps
1

Choose Your Payment Method

Browse 54+ options — mobile money, crypto, bank transfers, or digital wallets. Filter by country or category. Each method shows the agent, fee %, and a live countdown timer showing how long they've been active.

2

Get Payment Details

You'll see the exact account number/wallet address to send to, your unique transaction reference (VP-XXXX), and a step-by-step guide for your chosen method. The reference MUST appear in your payment notes.

3

Send & Upload Proof

Complete your payment externally (in your bank app, mobile money, or crypto wallet). Then upload a screenshot and enter your sender name. Both are required — this protects you and the agent.

4

VendorPlus Verifies & Confirms

Your agent is notified on VendorPlus and confirms receipt. You'll see a live status update on screen. If not confirmed within 15 minutes, it automatically escalates to admin. A downloadable receipt is generated on confirmation.

⚡ What makes VendorPlus different?

  • Every agent is verified and approved directly by VendorPlus before handling any transactions
  • Screenshot proof is mandatory — no proof, no confirmation
  • Your transaction reference auto-generates and tracks your payment end-to-end
  • If anything goes wrong, you get escalation to admin automatically
  • Full downloadable receipt on every confirmed transaction
Terms & FAQ
Policies, rules, and answers to common questions

📋 Platform Rules

  • Always include your VendorPlus reference (VP-XXXX) in the payment narration/memo
  • Screenshot proof is mandatory. Screenshots must clearly show the amount, recipient, and date
  • Sender name must match the name on your payment exactly
  • Payments must be completed within the 30-minute timer window shown on each method
  • VendorPlus is not responsible for payments sent to incorrect details copied incorrectly

💸 Fee Policy

Platform fees range from 0.8% to 3.5% depending on the payment method. Fees are shown clearly on each method card before you select it. Fees are non-refundable once a transaction is confirmed.

🔄 Refunds & Disputes

  • If your payment is confirmed but the intended service isn't delivered, contact your agent within 24 hours
  • Disputes must include your VP reference, screenshot proof, and sender name
  • Refund decisions are made within 48–72 hours by admin
  • We do not handle refunds for payments sent without the correct VP reference
❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What if I send money but forget to include the reference? +
Contact your agent immediately via the chat feature. Include your sender name, amount, and time of transfer. The agent can manually verify — but this may take longer and is not guaranteed.
The timer expired — can I still send? +
No. An expired timer means the agent's account may no longer be active. Return to Step 1 and select a method with an active timer. Starting a new session generates a new reference.
How long does confirmation take? +
Most agents confirm within 5–15 minutes. If the agent doesn't respond within 15 minutes, the transaction automatically escalates to admin. You'll see a status update in Step 4.
Is my screenshot stored securely? +
Your screenshot is only shared with the assigned agent for verification. It is not stored permanently on public servers.
What currencies are supported? +
We support KES, UGX, NGN, GHS, USD, GBP, EUR, XOF, TZS, ZAR, INR and all major cryptocurrencies. The currency depends on the payment method you select.
I sent to the wrong account. What do I do? +
Contact support immediately using the Help button (bottom right of this page). Include your VP reference, amount, and proof. We will do our best to assist but cannot guarantee recovery for incorrect transfers.
